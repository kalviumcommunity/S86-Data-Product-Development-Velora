# KPI Reference

## Monthly Active Users (MAU)
Definition: Distinct customers with at least one transaction in the last 30 days.
Formula: COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id) WHERE transaction_date >= TODAY() - 30 days
Data Source: transactions table (customer_id, transaction_date)
Target Range: 5,000 - 6,000
Owner: Product Manager
Update Frequency: Daily
Notes: Indicator of product engagement; seasonal dips in Q4.

## Revenue per Customer
Definition: Average revenue generated by each unique customer.
Formula: SUM(amount) / COUNT(DISTINCT customer_id)
Data Source: transactions table (customer_id, amount)
Target Range: $90 - $110
Owner: Finance Lead
Update Frequency: Daily
Notes: Helps track monetization efficiency and segment mix.

## Churn Rate
Definition: Share of customers active in the previous period but absent in the current period.
Formula: Customers active in period 1 but not period 2 / Customers active in period 1
Data Source: transactions table (customer_id, transaction_date)
Target Range: 0% - 5%
Owner: Customer Success Manager
Update Frequency: Weekly
Notes: Rising churn usually requires retention action or product review.

## Payment Success Rate
Definition: Share of payment attempts that complete successfully.
Formula: Successful payments / Total payment attempts
Data Source: payments or transactions table (status or payment_status)
Target Range: 95% - 100%
Owner: Engineering Manager
Update Frequency: Daily
Notes: A payment dip often indicates gateway, UX, or fraud issues.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
Definition: Average cost to acquire one new customer.
Formula: Total acquisition spend / Newly acquired customers
Data Source: marketing spend table and new customer attribution data
Target Range: $0 - $50
Owner: Growth Manager
Update Frequency: Weekly
Notes: Should be read alongside revenue per customer and payback period.

## Average Order Value (AOV)
Definition: Average value of a completed transaction.
Formula: SUM(amount) / COUNT(transactions)
Data Source: transactions table (amount)
Target Range: $75 - $125
Owner: Product Manager
Update Frequency: Daily
Notes: Useful for measuring basket size and upsell success.
